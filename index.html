Intro

Based in South Florida - I take a measured, analytical approach to every project, balancing high level strategy with attention to detail. Experienced in working with corporate accounts, athletes, high profile clients, and UHNWI's. Odds are, if it needs to get done, I can make it happen. Check out my Work.

Work

Private Aviation. As an advisor with Exclusive Charter Service, I coordinate the full spectrum of private aviation: sea planes, helicopters, turbo props, piston aircraft, very light jets, light jets, midsize and heavy jets, ultra long range jets, and even commercial airliners. This breadth of access lets me match the right equipment to each client’s trip - business, family, or leisure.

Boat & Superyacht Charters. Alongside aviation, I partner with Exclusive Charter Service’s sister company, Yachts Worldwide, to arrange boat and superyacht charters ranging from single day escapes to extended itineraries - all with trusted operators and carefully selected vessels.

Markets & Strategy. Drawing on my background in finance, I stay actively engaged in research and strategy, applying a data driven approach to markets.
